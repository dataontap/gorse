About dot eSIM

Company Information

Data On Tap Inc. is a Canadian independent connectivity provider operating under network code 302 100. We are a CRTC licensed Full MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator) founded in 2018 in Parkdale, ON, Canada.

Our Mission

We provide affordable and awesome connectivity solutions for Canadians and global travelers. Our mission is to deliver seamless, high-quality mobile data services that keep you connected wherever you are.

What We Offer

  • Global connectivity with no-expiry mobile data
  • Infinitely shareable data plans
  • Fast 5G+ network coverage
  • Easy eSIM activation in under 1 minute
  • Specialized services for Canadian users

Our Technology

As a Full MVNO, we operate our own core network infrastructure while partnering with established carriers to provide comprehensive coverage. Our eSIM technology allows for instant activation and seamless switching between networks as you travel.

Regulatory Compliance

Data On Tap Inc. is fully licensed by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) as a Full Mobile Virtual Network Operator. We operate under strict regulatory guidelines to ensure reliable and compliant telecommunications services.
